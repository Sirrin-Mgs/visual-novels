Auren Kwangila

chapter 9

Aranar babu wanda yayi barcin kirki cikinsu, musanmman Saudart data kusan kwana tana kuka. Da asuba a kasalance ta tashi tayi Sallah ko lazimi Kasa yi tayi barci ya dauketa akan sallayar saboda rashin isashen barci datayi jiya. Sai 8:00am ta farka, wanka tashiga Bayan yan mintuna tafito sannan tayi shirin aiki a kasalance, har yanzu takasa yarda da abinda yafaru jiya. Ahankali take saukowa a stairs din hannunta daya da waya danyen kuma rataye da side bag. Dago kai yayi yakai dubanshi saitinda yajiyo takunta. Kallo daya yayi mata yagane kukan datayi jiya ba kadan bane saboda yada idanun suka dan kumbura gashi basu ida washewaba. Tunda suka hada idanu ta kauda kai sannan tanufoshi a kasalance. "Ina kwana Dad" ta gaisheshi in a formal way. Kamo hannunta yayi zai zaunar daita kusadashi ta janye cikin wayancewa. "Dad, am late, zan wuce aiki" Kara kamo hannunta yayi yace "break fa, baki karya ba" "Am ok" ta amsa ataqaice. "No, dole kici wani Abu, please sit down" Zata bude baki tayi musu yayi saurin katseta da "Please.." Babu musu ta ja kujerar ta zauna, da kanshi yayi serving dinta sannan ya turamata gabanta. Ba musu tajawo abincin tashiga ci Saidai Duk yadda yaso su hada ido taqi yarda. Tutturawa taitayi har saida taci rabi sannan ta ture plate din tana goge bakinta da tissue. Kamo hannunta guda yayi ganin tana niyyar tashi hakan yasa ta fasa tashin Saidai ko dagowa batayiba. "Shalele?.... Fushi kike da Daddynki?" Shiru tayi kanta Kasa "Am so sorry shalele, banyi hakan don 6atamiki raiba. Kawai banida za6ine, shalele ko jiya saida akayiman barazana ta waya, ga banki kema kifini sanin halinda nake ciki Basai nasake miki bayaniba" yafada a tausashe yana murza hannunta. Har yanzu shiru tayi batace komai ba kuma bata dagoba. "Shalele idan da inada yadda zanyi nima Tun farko zanyi rejecting dinsu Amma a halin yanzu they are my last hope" "Dan ba'aso mutum na fidda rai akan rahmar ubangiji, arziqi da rashinshi Duk na Allah ne, saboda bamason talauci saimu sa6ama Allah? Shin wayau zamu yima Allah? Kasani Allah yasan damu Kuma yasan halinda muke ciki, akwai wadanda suka fimu shiga matsala kuma sun dauki qaddara, why not Muma muyi koyi dasu? Dad wannan kwata kwata is not an option, haram ne" "Shalele, Allah na yafewa Duk Wanda ya nemi yafiyarshi, zamu nemi yafiyar Allah Bayan mungama komai, Allah gafurun raheem ne"

Shiru Saudart tayi don takaici yama hanata magana "Say something please jewel" Janye hannunta tayi Daga riqonshi tace "Dad bazan iyaba, tunda bazaka iya rungumar qaddara nizan siyarda bakery dina saikayi settling din komai, inyaso saina nemi aiki" Da ada ne zai yarda Amma yanzu babu abinda yakeso kamar yazama surukin A.A so maganar siyarda bakery ma be tasoba. "Shalele siyarda bakery dinki won't help, kawai amincewarki ne kadai zai taimakeni" "Then am sorry, bazan iyaba" tafada ta tashi afusace zata wuce. Kamo hannunta yayi da sauri tareda juyota da d'an karfi "Don't you dare walk out on me ok?! Ina miki magana zaki wani tashi min ki wuce, ni tsaranki ne?!" Yafada 6acin ran dayake dannewa yaqi dannuwa. Kallonshi take idanunta suka kawo ruwa don tunda take beta6a yimata maganaba cikin wannan yanayin. "Dad?.." "It ok, kina iya tafiya, atunanina ke mai sharemin hawayena ce, a tunanina ke mai rufamin asiri ce but am made a mistake!" "Dad..." Saikuma ta fashe da kuka. "Go! Kawai ki tafi nagode" yafada yana barin parlourn Dama gidan gabadaya. Kuka Saudart keyi kamar ranta zai fita, tarasa gane meyasa idanun daddynta yarufe har haka. Bakeryn dabata fitaba kenan, ranar taci kuka har ta godewa Allah. Saidai tayima 'yan bakery dinta qaryar batajin dadi sannan ta wakilta P.A dinta tayi taking charge. Yini tayi cikin qunci ranar tayita jiran dawowar daddy Amma hartayi barci be dawoba. Washegari ma Tun kafin tafito yafita. Duniya tayima Saudart zafi don kwata kwata Dad yafita harkarta. Abinda be ta6a yi mataba, kullum cikin kuka take don bema barin su hadu, koda sun hadu baya amsa gaisuwarta barema azarce sa magana, Dama yaganta zai fice ko yashige dakinshi beqara fitowa. Dukda damuwar datake ciki na shariyar daddy hakan be sata jin zata amince da buqatarshi ba, she's stubborn, tagado taurin kan maminta ne. Yau Monday dawuri tashirya cikin wata gown din atamfa mai A, sannan ta yane kanta da kyale mai dan girma. Sosai tayi kyau dukda kallo daya zaka yimata kasan tana cikin damuwa. Fitowa tayi falo, kamar yadda tayi zato Dad yariga yafita. Itama Sakai tayi tafita batareda ta kula breakfast da'aka jeramataba. Motarta tashiga sannan ta karya motar hanyar company din A.A ENTERPRISES. Dole ayita yau taqare, tagaji da attitude da daddynta kebata. Bazata iya juraba kuma, dolene yau taga A.A.
